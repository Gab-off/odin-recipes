Tater Tot Casserole

Description

This Tater Tot casserole is a quick and easy dinner that everyone will love. Just four basic ingredients come together for this comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Canned Soup
  • Seasonings
  • Tater tots
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beefs, then stir in the soup and Seasonings
  2. Tranfer the beef to a baking dish. Top with tater tots, then cheese.
  3. Bake until the tots are golden brown.
